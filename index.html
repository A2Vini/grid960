A simple jQuery Plugin to add a 960 grid to your page

Press "alt + g" to toggle the grid.
Simply call the plugin with or without options (default: 12 columns, 20px gutter, 25px row height) 

$(function(){
    $('body').grid960();
});
                
$(function(){
    $('body').grid960(
        columns: 12,
        gutter: 20,
        row: 25
    );
});
